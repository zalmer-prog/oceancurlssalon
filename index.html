Who We Are

A little salon with a big heart by the sea

Ocean Curls Salon is a group of women who genuinely love what we do — and it shows. We believe great hair should feel as good as it looks, so we take our time, listen closely, and treat every guest like a friend who dropped by.

Whether you're after a subtle refresh or a head-turning makeover, you'll leave feeling like the most confident version of yourself. Comfy chairs, good conversation, and hair you can't stop touching — that's the Ocean Curls way.

  • Personalized consultations, every visit
  • Color, curls & cuts by a team of specialists
  • A warm, welcoming, women-led space
